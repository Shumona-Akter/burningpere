How to bye

Create wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

Get some BNB

Have BNB in your wallet to switch to $BURNINGPEPE. If you don’t have any BNB, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

Go to PancakeSwap

Connect to PancakeSwap. Go to pancakeswap.finance in google chrome or on the browser inside your Metamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $BURNINGPEPE token address into PancakeSwap, select BURNING PEPE, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.

Swap BNB for
Burning Pepe

Switch BNB for $BURNINGPEPE. We have ZERO taxes so you don’t need to worry about buying with a specific slippage, although you may need to use slippage during times of market volatility.
